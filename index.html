The Problem

​The Syrian conflict has left children parentless, without basic necessities such as food, clothing, shelter, medical care, and education.

7.6 MILLION CHILDREN IN NEED

Directly as a result of the Syrian conflict, 7.6 million children are in need of clean water, food, shelter, schooling and medical supplies 

5.6 MILLION CHILDREN IN DIRE SITUATIONS

5.6 million children are without a permanent place of residence, constantly living in dangerous situations. 

2.0 MILLION CHILDREN IN DIFFICULT TO REACH AREAS

A difficult to reach area is an area that is inaccessible to aid workers. 

OUR MISSION

Our aim is to raise funds and awareness through a grassroots campaign to aid Syrian orphans in need.
