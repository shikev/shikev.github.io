September 29th, 7 PM: The White Helmets Screening

To kick off the school year, we, along with Unicef, are cosponsoring Books Not Bombs' event this upcoming Thursday, September 29th at 7 PM.
We will be presenting a screening of The White Helmets, a movie about the unsung heroes of Syria. Click here for the trailer!



There is a suggested admission fee of $3, all of which will go to directly aiding the Syrian orphans.
