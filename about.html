The Team

Hey! My name is Farhan and I am a sophomore studying BCN and Near Eastern/Islamic Studies here at Michigan. You can usually catch me in the CCRB in the wee hours of the night playing some ball with my roommates. If not, then I would most likely be eating Suvai Palace lunch buffet or some good old No Thai. I enjoy cooking in my free time, and surprisingly a huge cat person (I have one of my own). Listening to the injustices committed by Assad's regime and ISIS in this once beautiful area of the levant reminded me of the first-hand accounts from a close family friend. She mentioned her time in Bosnia during the ethnic cleaning of Bosniac Muslims in the early 1990's, and since then I grew frustrated at the injustice and oppression happening all over the world. As a way to help diminish part of the suffering, we wanted to create a means to which we can help provide to those who were once enjoying healthy lives, but now are stuck in an unfortunate situation. We founded SOSA so we as college students can play an important role by helping the lives of orphans who have lost their parents during the war.
